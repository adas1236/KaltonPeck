\begin{abstract}
This blueprint organizes the proof of the Kalton--Peck rank-parity obstruction into a
dependency-tracked mathematical roadmap.  It develops the algebraic parity argument,
Banach--Fredholm infrastructure, a bounded mod-$2$ path theorem, and the special symplectic and
operator-theoretic properties of the real Kalton--Peck space.  The main consequences are that
$T^2+I$ has even rank whenever it has finite rank on $Z_2$, and that no hyperplane of $Z_2$
admits a complex structure.
\end{abstract}

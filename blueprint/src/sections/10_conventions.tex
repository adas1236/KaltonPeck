\section{Conventions}
All vector spaces and Banach spaces below are real.  For a Banach space $X$, its continuous dual is
denoted by $\strongdual X$.  If $S:X\to\strongdual X$ is induced by an alternating form, its
radical is $\ker S$.  For a closed subspace $E\subseteq X$ of finite codimension,
$\codim_X E$ means $\dim(X/E)$.  All dimensions that occur in congruences are finite real
dimensions.  Congruences without an indicated modulus are modulo $2$.
